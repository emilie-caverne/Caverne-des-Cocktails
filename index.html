Cosmopolitan

2 oz vodka, 1 oz cranberry juice, 1/2 oz lime juice, 1 oz orange juice, and a dash of grenadine.

Mix all ingredients together in a shaker filled with ice. Shake well, then strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with a lime wheel and a strawberry if desired.

Mojito

1 oz light rum, 4 oz sugar, 2 cups ice, 2 lime slices, 1 mint sprig, and soda water.

In a tall glass filled with ice, combine sugar, rum, and lime slices. Muddle well. Add more ice, the remaining ingredients, and shake well. Strain into a tall glass filled with ice and serve with a straw.

Old Fashioned

2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey, 1 oz simple syrup, and 1/2 oz bitters.

In a rocks glass filled with ice, add bourbon or rye whiskey and stir. Add the simple syrup and stir until completely dissolved. Add the bitters and stir. Garnish with a orange twist and serve.
